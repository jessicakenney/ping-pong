Ping Pong

Let's Play!

Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following additions:

  • 1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  • 2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  • 3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "pingpong"

3 and 5 are the default divisors. Try your own if you'd like.
